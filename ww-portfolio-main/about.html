Mission & Vision:

RAMP aims to bridge the gap for non-native English speaking professionals, startsups, and small to mid-size companies, by providing high-quality, industry-specific practical Business English skills training that is accessible only to corporations with dedicated internal trainers.

RAMP's mission is to unlock the potential of every business and employees we work with, helping them thrive in today’s competitive global market.

By focusing on the needs of your specific industry, and delivering customized, research-backed training, RAMP strives to save you time and money, and become the go-to service for business English communication skills training in the Asia-Pacific region.
